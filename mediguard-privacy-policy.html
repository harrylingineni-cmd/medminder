Privacy Policy for MediGuard

Last updated: 15 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the MediGuard mobile application ("MediGuard", "the app", "we", "us") handles your information. MediGuard is a medication reminder app that helps people remember to take their medications on time. Please read this policy carefully. By using MediGuard, you agree to the practices described here.

In short: MediGuard stores the information you enter only on your own device. We do not have a server, we do not collect your personal data, we do not track you, and we do not share your information with anyone.

Information the App Stores

To do its job, MediGuard lets you enter and stores the following information locally on your device:

  • The names of your medications
  • Their dosages
  • The times you want to be reminded
  • Your reminder settings (such as how long reminders should repeat)
  • Records of whether you have marked a dose as taken or snoozed

This information is saved only in the app's local storage on your phone. It is not sent to us or to any other company or server.

Information We Do NOT Collect

MediGuard does not:

  • Require you to create an account or log in
  • Collect your name, email address, phone number, or location
  • Send your medication information to any server (the app has no server)
  • Use analytics, advertising, or tracking tools
  • Share, sell, or transfer your information to third parties

Permissions the App Uses

MediGuard requests certain phone permissions only so that reminders work correctly:

  • Notifications — to show you reminder alerts at your chosen times.
  • Alarms & reminders (exact alarms) — so reminders appear at the precise scheduled time.

These permissions are used only to display reminders on your device. They are not used to collect or transmit any information.

How Your Information Is Stored and Deleted

All of your information stays on your device. Because nothing is stored on a server, you are always in control:

  • You can delete any medication from within the app at any time.
  • Uninstalling the app removes all of the information it has stored on your device.

Children's Privacy

MediGuard is intended for use by adults, including older adults and caregivers. It is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Medical Disclaimer

MediGuard is a reminder tool only. It is not a medical device, and it does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. It does not guarantee that a medication has been taken, and it is not a substitute for professional medical care or the advice of a doctor or pharmacist. Always follow the instructions of your healthcare provider and check the medication label before taking any medication. In an emergency, contact your local emergency services.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the app develops and new features are added. If we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. If future versions of the app collect or share information differently, this policy will be updated to describe that before those features are released.
